About me

Hello, my name is Liam Gilmartin. I am a senior at University of Hartford. I am a cinema major. I'm from Southampton New York. I was born in Sacramento California and lived there during my early childhood. The reason I decided to take this class was because I had originally planned on minoring in Computer Science but decided against it after a few classes since it wasn't really my thing. I figured web design would be better for me since it offers more creativity over traditional software development.

For years now, photography has been one of my biggest hobbies. I got my first camera when I was thirteen years old. Most of my photos are landscape and wildlife photos. I've always loved finding new places and seeing new things. Although I have no plans on making a career out of it, I believe it will remain an important part of my life for years to come.
